Today’s trending data shows a clear concentration on **AI agents and their infrastructure**. Coding agents dominate the top‑gainers: DeepSeek‑TUI, agent‑skills, and goose all center on making AI assistants more capable inside developer environments. The high star counts reflect the community’s hunger for terminal‑first, local‑first tools that offer real utility today.

The second notable cluster is **RAG and retrieval innovation**. Inference optimizations (dflash) and gateway/proxy solutions (9router, InsForge) round out the landscape. Notably, there are no new model releases or fine‑tuning breakthroughs in today’s trending, but the topic search reveals sustained interest in LLM training from scratch (minimind, bit‑brain) and pretraining libraries (stable‑pretraining).

Overall, the ecosystem is maturing: **agents are being productized** with skill libraries, terminal UIs, and flexible backends, while **RAG is evolving** toward lighter, more storage‑efficient architectures.
