Developers are moving beyond basic agent frameworks to fine-grained control over agent persona, tone, and capabilities.

- **Local-First AI Inference** — The continued high stars of `ollama` (172K+) and `open-webui` (139K+) combined with new educational content like `tiny-llm` indicates sustained community investment in local, private, and educational LLM deployment — foundational for both hobbyists and privacy-conscious enterprises.
