- **Agent infrastructure dominates today’s trending**: Four of the top five trending repos by new stars are directly related to agent development—`headroom` (token optimization), `ECC` (agent harness), `hermes-agent`, and `hermes-webui`. This reflects a growing focus on making agents more efficient and easier to deploy.

The topic search also shows `thedotmack/claude-mem` (80.5k stars) and `topoteretes/cognee` (17.7k stars), indicating a sustained community push to solve cross-session context.

- **PDF/document parsing for AI pipelines sees new entries**: `opendataloader-pdf` (+570 today) and `microsoft/markitdown` (+1984 today) both focus on converting unstructured documents into AI-friendly formats. This aligns with the broader RAG ecosystem, where high-quality parsing is a recurring bottleneck.

- **Low-resource LLM inference continues to draw interest**: `airllm` (+208 today) and the earlier `minimind` (51k total) highlight community efforts to democratize LLM usage on consumer hardware.
