## Hot List Observations

**Agent ecosystems dominate today's activity.** The agent / workflow category accounts for the largest share of high-star projects in both the trending list and topic searches. Projects like **hermes-agent**, **dify**, **OpenHands**, and **CherryHQ** represent a mature ecosystem of agent frameworks, while newer projects like **goose** (a "code agent beyond suggestions") push this space further.

**Skill and "agent marketplace" projects are a new signal.** The simultaneous emergence of **last30days-skill**, **taste-skill**, and the broader skill-oriented projects in topic searches (like **CopilotKit**, **claude-mem**) suggests an emerging pattern: treating AI agents as platforms with interchangeable skills, memory modules, and quality filters. This is distinct from monolithic agent frameworks.

**Infrastructure gets a fresh wave.** **turbovec** (high-performance vector index in Rust) and **pg_durable** (Postgres embedded durable execution) both made the trending list today with strong star counts. These represent two critical infrastructure layers — vector storage and agent state management — being re-architected for AI-native workloads.

**Notable: The "offline AI" angle.** **project-nomad** (a self-contained offline survival computer with AI) and strong continued interest in **ollama** / **llama.cpp** indicate sustained community interest in local / edge AI scenarios.
