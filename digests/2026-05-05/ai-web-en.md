#### Product Announcement: Claude Opus 4.7

**Published:** 2026-05-04
**Link:** https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-7

Claude Opus 4.7 represents a meaningful step forward in Anthropic's model progression, with the company positioning it as particularly suited for the "hardest coding work" that previously required close human supervision. The model demonstrates enhanced capabilities across several dimensions:

- **Software Engineering Excellence:** Opus 4.7 shows substantial improvements on advanced software engineering tasks compared to Opus 4.6, with users reporting confidence in delegating complex, long-running tasks without continuous oversight.
- **Instruction Adherence and Verification:** The model exhibits rigorous attention to instructions and has been designed to devise ways to verify its own outputs before reporting—indicating Anthropic's continued investment in reliability for production use cases.
- **Vision Capabilities:** Substantially improved vision allows the model to process images at greater resolution, expanding its utility for multimodal enterprise applications.
- **Creative and Professional Tasks:** Enhanced "taste" and creativity produce higher-quality interfaces, slides, and documentation, positioning Opus 4.7 as a stronger contender for knowledge work automation.
- **Cyber Safeguards Implementation:** Notably, Opus 4.7 is the first model to incorporate safeguards developed from lessons learned during the Claude Mythos Preview project. Anthropic is explicitly testing new cyber safeguards on this less capable model before applying them to more advanced systems, demonstrating a tiered approach to safety deployment.

**Strategic Significance:** The explicit comparison to "Claude Mythos Preview" and the discussion of differential capability reduction for cyber capabilities signals Anthropic's serious engagement with AI security concerns. The model is positioned as a workhorse for serious engineering tasks rather than a frontier capability demonstration.
